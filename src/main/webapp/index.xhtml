Welcome to the shop. Click on the products link to browse our inventory.

Computers are our passion here at PC Parts Online. Browse our inventory and find the parts to build the machine of your dreams. All products are sold at MSRP or lower. We take our passion for the hobby serious and require every user to have an account in order to make a purchase, or to browse the wares.

This shop was started in order to help people get the parts that they want. Due to the COVID-19 supply chain issues, many people took advantage of it by purchasing the hardware at normal prices, then would sell them third-party for a profit. After seeing this happen for many months, we decided to open our own shop to help others make the machine that they always wanted, and at a fair price.
